Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: 30/06/2021

These are the questions which are too commonly asked by the customer.

  • 1. What is Jusbid?

    Jusbid is an OTA platform with a unique bidding module to make hotel and flight booking a seamless experience for customers, travel partners, and hoteliers. We offer a common platform for both parties to bargain and accommodate a reasonable price transparently and securely.

  • 2. In how much time will the hotel be booked on Jusbid?

    You can complete this process in 30 minutes.

  • 3. What are the valid ID Proofs that I should carry?

    You need to carry a valid government-issued address ID proof with a photo of the address. Some of the valid id proofs are: Driving License Voter ID Card Aadhar Card. Passport PAN Cards are NOT Valid as ID Proofs.

  • 4. If I cancel the hotel booking then how much time amount will return?

    If you cancel the hotel booking then It Depends on the hotel cancellation policy.

Payments For Booking

  • 1. Do you charge any fees for your services?

    No, jusbid.in doesn’t charge any fees for its services. Our service is completely free of charge and we will not add to your accommodation any additional fees for our service.

  • 2. Why do you require my credit card details for bookings?

    jusbid.in acts as an intermediary between our clients and hotels. Your credit card details are required by hotels in most cases as a guarantee for your reservation in case of late cancellation or “no-show”. The hotels have a right to preauthorize your credit card so please make sure that your credit card is valid. Also please note, that if you book a Non-refundable room, this type of room has special conditions and your credit card will be charged in advance. Please, always double-check the payment and cancellation policy of the room you are interested in before you proceed with the reservation.

  • 3. Is it secure to send my credit card details?

    Yes, it is secure as we are using encrypted SSL protocol in all our order forms. that is the latest, most secure technology that encrypts all the details provided in the order form includes your credit card details.

  • 4. How do I pay for our accommodation?

    You can pay for your accommodation via Net Banking / UPI Payment.

Booking Creation

  • 1. How do I book my accommodation?

    Booking is possible only by completing the reservation form on our website. Please, select the dates of your stay and the type of rooms you have chosen. The system will show you the price for the accommodation (always check the room conditions). If you agree with the total price and the booking conditions, just click on the red button "Book now" and the reservation form will show up. Once you finish the order form and click on the button "Book now", your reservation will be confirmed shortly to your email address.

  • 2. How do I know that my booking is confirmed?

    After completing and sending us your order form, you will get an automatic receipt email containing your order number. Within 30 minutes, you will receive Accommodation confirmation. The Accommodation confirmation serves you as a voucher, so please carry it with you as it contains all the vital details (address of your hotel, contact phone numbers, and other instructions). In case that you requested in your order form any special requests – extra bed, non-smoking room, parking, etc.

  • 3. Can I book your services by telephone, email, or fax?

    Unfortunately not. If you wish to make a booking, please fill in the order form on our website.

  • 4. I made a reservation but did not receive any confirmation. What should I do?

    Before finalizing your reservation please double-check your e-mail address because a mistake in the e-mail address is the most common reason why our clients do not receive their confirmations. In some cases, our e-mails can also end up in the “spam folder” so please check it as well. In any case, please try to contact us by e-mail or call center as we will be very happy to assist you and solve the problem as soon as possible.

  • 5. How many rooms can I book at once?

    You can book as many rooms as are available for the selected period.

Booking Cancellation

  • 1. How do I cancel my booking?

    Please click on the link provided in the Accommodation confirmation and cancel your reservation.

  • 2. How do I know that the reservation was canceled?

    When your reservation is canceled, you will get a cancellation confirmation from us. If you don’t hear back from us in 30 minutes, please get in touch with us by some other means (e-mail pr call center).

  • 3. I made a cancellation but did not receive any confirmation. What should I do?

    Before finalizing your reservation, please double-check your e-mail address because a mistake in the e-mail address is the most common reason why our clients do not receive their confirmations. In some cases, our e-mails can also end up in the “spam folder” so please check it as well. In any case, please try to contact us by e-mail as we will be very happy to assist you and solve the problem as soon as possible.

  • 4. What cancellation conditions do you have? When at the latest can I change the booking without being charged any fee?

    Each room has its cancellation policy and for this information always check the room conditions. Generally, your reservation can be canceled or changed a few days before your arrival. However, there are some exceptions - for example, "Non-Refundable“ or "Special conditions“ rooms. You can check the room policy if you click on the room type.
